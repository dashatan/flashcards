## Definition

**Core Web Vitals** are Google’s key user-centric metrics: loading ([LCP](concept:lcp)), interactivity ([INP](concept:inp)), visual stability ([CLS](concept:cls)).

## Why it exists

Quantify real UX and SEO signals with field data (CrUX).

## How it works

Measured in the field (75th percentile). Lab tools: Lighthouse. RUM products track production users.

## In practice

Senior frontend performance interviews; tie optimizations to specific metrics.
