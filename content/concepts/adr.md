## Definition

An **ADR** documents a significant technical decision — context, options, decision, and consequences — in the repo.

## Why it exists

Future teams understand *why* not just *what*; reduces repeated debates.

## How it works

Short markdown: `docs/adr/001-use-tanstack-query.md`. Status: proposed, accepted, deprecated.

## In practice

Lightweight alternative to heavy design docs; pair with README for onboarding.
